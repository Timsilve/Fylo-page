Simple Omelette Recipe

an easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.this complete omelette meal combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with yiur choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total : Approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation : 5 minutes
  • cooking : 5 minutes

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:in a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a flutter texture
  2. Heat the pan:place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. cook the omelette:once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan ti ensure that the egss evenly coat the the surface
  4. Add fillings (optional):when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillingsiber one half of the omelette
  5. fold and serve:as the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. let it cook for another minute,then slide it into a plate
  6. Enjoy: serve hot,with additional salt amd pepper if needed
